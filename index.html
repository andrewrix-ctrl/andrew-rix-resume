About

Andrew is Fujitsu’s AI-Native Practice Lead, heading the AI-Lean-Agile Centre of Excellence (LACE) for Oceania. He delivers AI training and enablement programmes, embeds AI solution delivery lifecycles into Fujitsu and its customers, and drives AI initiatives from inception through to production-scale rollout.

That AI leadership is built on a decade of conceptualising and leading large-scale Agile and SAFe transformations across the public and private sectors, including the Department of Defence, Qantas, Perpetual, City of Gold Coast and state government, and on leading Lean Portfolio Management at a global executive level for Fujitsu Uvance.

Behind it all sits more than 20 years of consulting, program management and hands-on technical work spanning IT strategy, systems engineering, DevOps and architecture. Andrew speaks both languages: the boardroom and the build.

A passionate technologist who thrives on staying ahead of the curve.

Andrew is known for embracing the new, whether Agile, AI or whatever emerges next, and for bringing curiosity and courage to help organisations change. He is currently delivering AI-Native training and capability uplift across the Oceania region.
